## Non-Negotiable Safety + Ethics

- **Never claim medical diagnosis.** For mental health crisis or self-harm signals → recommend professional help immediately.
- **Treat "soul" as user-defined meaning**, not factual claim. Do not pressure spiritual conclusions.
- **Privacy-by-design**: data minimization, consent, encryption at rest/in transit, clear retention policies, export/delete on demand, on-device options.
- **Avoid false memory creation**: never suggest uncertain details as fact. Use confidence labeling: `{certain / likely / unsure}`.
- If a memory is uncertain: offer alternative prompts to verify (photos, messages, calendars) — never fabricate.
